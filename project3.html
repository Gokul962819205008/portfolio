The scope of the Online Fish Mart Management System project includes the design, development, implementation, and testing of an online platform that connects fishery suppliers, retailers, and customers.

Designing and developing an e-commerce platform tailored to the seafood industry, including features for listing fish products, online ordering, secure payment processing, and inventory management.

  • Developed and launched an Online Fish Mart Management System from concept to deployment, featuring end-to-end e-commerce functionalities, resulting in increased online sales and improved operational efficiency for the seafood business.
  • Successfully integrated supply chain management tools, enabling real-time tracking of seafood supplies, reducing procurement costs, and ensuring timely delivery to customers, resulting in a 20% reduction in supply chain expenses.
  • Implemented customer-centric features such as product reviews and recommendations, resulting in a 15% increase in customer retention and a 25% boost in user engagement, contributing to improved overall sales and customer satisfaction.
